About us:
We're a locally owned Medical Company in Jackson, TN that buys, fix, and cleans medical equipment to be resold.

We are open every day of the week from 8:00am till 7:00pm!

The Master Medical Equipment Promise: If there is a product that does not pass our MME 5-Point Inspection process, it does not receive the MME Seal of Approval. We offer that guarantee because we want only the best for our customers. Don’t be persuaded by the one-man popshops out there with used medical equipment who promise more than they can deliver. Our company stands by our guarantee and if we don’t have what you need, we can find it from a reputable dealer. Here at Master Medical Equipment, we value relationships and your business is important to us.
